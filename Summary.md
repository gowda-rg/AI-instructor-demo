This table breaks down the different types of prompts, focusing on their **primary function** and **distinctive feature**.

| **Prompt Type**              | **Distinction**                                                                |
| ---------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **1. Question**              | Requests specific information or clarification.                                |
| **2. Command**               | Instructs the AI to perform a specific action.                                 |
| **3. Query**                 | Seeks to retrieve specific data or results, often from a database.             |
| **4. Statement**             | Provides context or information without asking for a response.                 |
| **5. Inquiry**               | Explores a topic, often broader and more open-ended than a question.           |
| **6. Request**               | Politely asks for something to be done or created.                             |
| **7. Explanation**           | Seeks a detailed explanation or description of a concept.                      |
| **8. Scenario/Hypothetical** | Presents a "what-if" situation to explore outcomes.                            |
| **9. Creative Generation**   | Requests the AI to generate original, creative content (e.g., stories, poems). |
| **10. Role-Based Prompt**    | Assigns a specific role to the AI, e.g., expert, interviewer.                  |
| **11. Clarification**        | Seeks further explanation to clear up ambiguity or confusion.                  |
| **12. Feedback Prompt**      | Asks for an evaluation or improvement suggestions.                             |
| **13. Comparison**           | Requests a comparison or contrast between two or more items.                   |
| **14. Multi-step Prompt**    | Guides through a series of tasks or steps to reach a goal.                     |
| **15. System Prompt**        | Internal instructions for the AI to guide its behavior or tone.                |
